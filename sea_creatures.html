Deep-Sea Creatures

There are 40 species of deep-sea creatures you can catch in New Horizons. You catch them by swimming in the sea with a bathing suit and diving underwater. Any species you catch will appear in your Critterpedia. You can sell deep-sea creatures or donate each species to the Museum.

The following table shows all the types of deep-sea creatures available, when to catch them and how much you can sell them for. Note! The times and months given are based on the Northern Hemisphere.

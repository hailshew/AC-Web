Bugs

There are 80 species of bugs you can catch in New Horizons. Any bug species you catch will appear in your Critterpedia. You can sell fish or donate each species to the Museum, though Blathers will not be very happy to receive a bug!

The following table shows all the types of bug available, where and when to catch them and how much you can sell them for. Note! The times and months given are based on the Northern Hemisphere.

Name Image Sale Price Location Time Months

Yellow Butterfly 160 Flying 4am-7pm Mar-Jun,Sep-Oct
Tiger Butterfly 240 Flying 4am-7pm Mar-Sep
Peacock Butterfly 2,500 Flying by hybrid flowers 4am-7pm Mar-Jun
Common Bluebottle 300 Flying 4am-7pm Apr-Aug
Paper Kite Butterfly 1,000 Flying 8am-7pm All year
Great Purple Emperor 3,000 Flying 4am-7pm May-Aug

Emperor Butterfly 4,000 Flying 5pm-8am Dec-Mar, Jun-Sep
